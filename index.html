❓ Help

How to use:

  • Settings — Add players, then tap "Start round"
  • Play — Select each player's score for the hole and tap "Save hole"
  • History — View and edit scores from any hole
  • Standings — See live rankings and total scores

Playing order: On the first hole, order follows the player list. On subsequent holes, the player with the best score from the previous hole plays first (lowest score leads).

Removing players: When you remove a player from Settings, their scores are preserved in History for reference.

🔒 Privacy & Data
  • All data is stored locally in your browser's Local Storage
  • No data is ever sent to any server — 100% offline
  • No cookies, no tracking, no analytics
  • Clearing your browser's Local Storage will delete all data
  • Data is not shared between devices or browsers
  • ⚠️ If you clear your browser cache/history, your scores will be lost
